Colombo

Located in the heart of the city, our Colombo branch serves as the main hub of Care Compass, offering a wide range of specialized medical services. With state-of-the-art diagnostic and treatment facilities, we ensure that patients receive the highest standard of healthcare. Our hospital is equipped with 24/7 emergency services, a modern radiology unit, and a well-equipped diagnostic laboratory. Patients can access specialist consultations in fields such as cardiology, neurology, gynecology, and orthopedics, ensuring comprehensive medical care. Additionally, our maternity and pediatric care services provide excellent support for expecting mothers and newborns. The Colombo branch also offers an online appointment booking system to enhance patient convenience.



Kandy

Situated in the cultural capital of Sri Lanka, our Kandy branch provides multi-specialty medical services tailored to meet the needs of the local community. This branch offers inpatient and outpatient services, physiotherapy, pediatric care, and a fully stocked pharmacy to ensure patients have access to essential treatments and medications. Our advanced lab testing services, including blood tests, hormone level assessments, and infectious disease screenings, help in the early diagnosis of various health conditions. The Kandy branch is designed to offer a patient-friendly environment, with comfortable inpatient rooms and a dedicated rehabilitation center for post-surgical recovery. Whether you require routine checkups or specialized treatments, our highly qualified medical team is here to provide compassionate care.

Badulla

The Badulla branch of Care Compass is dedicated to serving the Uva Province with high-quality healthcare solutions. Focused on community health and wellness, this branch specializes in general medicine, maternal healthcare, and pediatric services. Expecting mothers can benefit from ultrasound scanning, pregnancy monitoring, and expert gynecological care. Additionally, our branch offers vaccination and immunization programs for children and adults to promote long-term health. We also provide telemedicine services, allowing patients in remote areas to consult with doctors online and receive medical advice from the comfort of their homes. The Badulla branch is well-equipped to manage chronic diseases such as diabetes and hypertension, providing tailored diet and lifestyle guidance to help patients lead healthier lives.
